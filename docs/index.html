Burgundy Passports

The British government have confirmed that, following Brexit, British passports will be blue once again. Many of us are rather attached to our burgundy passports and want to hold on to them for as long as possible. On this page, we will share some advice on keeping your beloved burgundy passport.

The new blue passports will start to be issued from October 2019. So if you renew your passport (or apply for a new one) after that date, you'll get one of the blue ones. So if you renew in September 2019, you'll get a burgundy one that will last for ten years. So you'll be able to proudly flash your burgundy passport until September 2029.

But it's also worth pointing out that you can renew your passport up to nine months before it expires and the extra months will be added to the expory date for the replacement. So if your passport expires between October 2019 and June 2020, you could renew early and still get a burgundy replacement.

You can renew your passport or apply for your first passport online.
